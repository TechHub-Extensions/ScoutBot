🏷️ Legend

  • 🇳🇬 Nigeria — Open to Nigerian students locally
  • 🌍 International — Study/work abroad, open to Nigerians
  • 💰 Funded — Includes a stipend, scholarship money, or travel grant
  • 🎓 Postgraduate — Requires a Masters or PhD qualification
  • 📋 Rolling — No fixed deadline, applications accepted ongoing
  • 🔒 Closed — Deadline passed (archived, not shown in active list)

🌍 Who Is This For?

ScoutBot was built for Nigerian university students and recent graduates who:

  • Don't have time to check 20 websites every week
  • Miss opportunities because they weren't in the right WhatsApp group
  • Want funded international scholarships and don't know where to look
  • Are looking for internships in tech, engineering, finance, law, or medicine
