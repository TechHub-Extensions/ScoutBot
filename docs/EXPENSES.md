## Notes

ScoutBot was built intentionally on free infrastructure to demonstrate that AI-native operations at meaningful scale are achievable with zero budget. The fundraising goal of ₦2,000,000 is intended for future Twitter/X advertising to expand the subscriber base from 500+ to 4,000+ Nigerian students — this has not been spent and is not revenue.

All 500+ subscribers were acquired organically through word-of-mouth, Nigerian student WhatsApp groups, and repository stargazers who shared the subscription form.
